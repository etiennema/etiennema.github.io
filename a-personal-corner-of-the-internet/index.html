    A Personal Corner of the Internet

    January 31, 2022
    rambles, internet

    I’ve been feeling like I don’t have a place on the internet anymore. It’s become a mall, homogenized by the Facebooks, Instagrams, Twitters, and YouTubes. Everything has a similar aesthetic. Everything is geared towards likes and follows.

    I miss the land of Geocities, and Xanga, and even MySpace, where pockets of the internet were weirder and more individualized. We weren’t so focused on building audiences or brands; we just made things because it was kind of amazing that we had this new canvas for expression.

    That’s what this blog is. An attempt to stake out my personal corner of the internet. No likes, no follows, no analytics. Just a record of what I’m working on and what’s been interesting to me. And maybe it’ll be interesting to you too—no pressure though.
